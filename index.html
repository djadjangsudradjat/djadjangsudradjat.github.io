Executive Summary

H. Djadjang Sudradjat is an aerospace technical professional with extensive experience in aircraft electrical installation, avionics support, testing, troubleshooting, harness installation, mock-up development, and aircraft maintenance. His primary career was built at PT Dirgantara Indonesia for more than 28 years, with involvement in aircraft programs such as NC-212, CN-235, N-250, N-230, and multiple technical assignments both in Indonesia and abroad.

  • More than 28 years of experience in the aerospace industry and aircraft electrical systems.
  • Experienced in installation, functional testing, troubleshooting, and integration of aircraft electrical & avionics systems.
  • Has worked across domestic and international projects, including Singapore, South Korea, Italy, Malaysia, and Burkina Faso.
